# Automotive Battery Sales vs Warranty Claims Analysis (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **automotive battery sales versus warranty claims** to identify trends, risks, and cost drivers.  
The dashboard helps businesses understand which products, suppliers, customers, or periods generate higher warranty claims.
> ‚ö†Ô∏è Note: The dataset used in this project is **anonymized and manipulated** for confidentiality purposes.
---
##  Objectives
- Compare battery sales volume against warranty claims
- Identify high-risk battery models and suppliers
- Detect customers with frequent warranty claims
- Analyze monthly and seasonal warranty patterns
- Support quality control and cost reduction decisions
---
##  Key Analysis Covered
- Sales vs Warranty Claims comparison
- Warranty claims by:
  - Battery item / model
  - Supplier / brand
  - Customer / dealer
  - Month and time period
- Warranty claim percentage by product
- Repeat and frequent claim identification
- High-risk suppliers and products analysis
---
##  Key Insights
- Certain battery models show **disproportionately high warranty claims**
- Specific suppliers contribute more to overall warranty costs
- Repeated claims observed from selected customers
- Warranty claims increase during certain months following peak sales
- Early indicators of quality, handling, or storage issues identified
---
##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI

##  Business Value
- Helps reduce warranty and replacement costs
- Improves supplier performance monitoring
- Assists in detecting abnormal or fraudulent claims
- Supports data-driven quality and inventory decisions
